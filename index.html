JP Deluxe Detailing , is a privately owned vehicle washing and detailing service located in the Nashua-Nh area. We offer high quality vehicle cleaning and detailing services at very competitive rates. We are in the business of providing the highest quality services and complete customer satisfaction.

In 2020, in the middle of a Pandemic, with a truly desire to bring a certain type of integrity and efficiency that competitors were lacking on car wash market in New England, me Joao Paulo and my friend Marcos took a risk and decided to partner together and start our own business, so that JP Deluxe Detailing got out of the paper and became real. We are both experienced car detailers and restoring and taking care of cars is our passion.
